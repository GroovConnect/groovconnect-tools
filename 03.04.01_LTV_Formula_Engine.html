LTV Formula Engine — T × M × R

Three inputs. All stated before any calculation. No spreadsheet. No hidden math.

LTV = T × M × R
Monthly Tuition × Gross Margin % × Months Retained
T
Monthly Tuition
Average gross revenue per student per month. Use actual ARPU, not advertised rate.
Default: operator-stated
M
Gross Margin %
Tuition remaining after direct costs (instructor pay, room costs).
Default: 50% if not stated
R
Months Retained
Average enrollment duration. Or estimate: 1 ÷ Monthly Churn Rate.
Default: pull from CRM

Kill Switch: trainee states a dollar figure without showing the formula derivation, or presents a gut-estimate without calculating. "Which formula produced that number? What are the three inputs?" A correct answer with hidden math receives zero credit in certification.
