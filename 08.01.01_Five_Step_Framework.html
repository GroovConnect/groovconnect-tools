Five-Step Communication Framework

The sequence is non-negotiable. The content within each step adapts. The sequence does not.

Lead with the conclusion. Every delivery follows: Observation → Insight → Impact → Recommendation → Action. If the operator doesn't understand in 30 seconds, the delivery failed — not the operator.
1
Observation
The specific, quantified KPI signal.
"Retention dropped from 85% to 71% over 60 days."
2
Insight
The diagnosed root cause.
"This is driven by a Standardization System failure — no lesson note framework exists."
3
Impact
The quantified economic consequence.
"This is creating approximately $44,000 in annual leakage."
4
Recommendation
The specific GC capability derived from the failure.
"We recommend installing the Instructor Intelligence layer with automated note submission gates."
5
Action
The specific next step with a timeline.
"Starting this week, with full rollout in 14 days."
Record every filler word and every pause. If delivery exceeds 30 seconds or contains more than 2 filler words — reset and repeat. A delivery that takes 90 seconds means the trainee is still assembling, not retrieving.
