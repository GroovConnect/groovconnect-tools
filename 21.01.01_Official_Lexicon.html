Official Lexicon — definitions of record

These are not working definitions — they are definitions of record. A trainee who uses a term in a way that diverges from its definition here is using the wrong term.

Precision in language is a component of diagnostic precision, not a stylistic preference. Ambiguity is the enemy of execution.
Core Diagnostic Terms
Attribution Error
The prohibited act of identifying a human as the root cause of an outcome before system layers have been fully audited. Every Attribution Error in a simulation triggers an automatic Kill Switch.
Causal Chain
The visual and logical map — built in Lucidchart to the Module 06 syntax standard — connecting a root cause to a KPI impact through behavior-change steps. A chain that ends before the KPI is incomplete. A chain that begins at a behavior rather than a system failure is a symptom map, not a causal chain.
Diagnostic Reflex
The trained, automatic ability to perceive a surface symptom and immediately begin tracing the propagation back to a system failure — without requiring a structured prompt or instructor guidance.
Drift
The natural, predictable tendency for a human-operated system to revert toward inconsistency over time without structural enforcement. Drift is not failure — it is the default state of an unmonitored system.
Kill Switch
A critical failure in logic or behavior during a simulation that results in an automatic failure and session pause. Six Kill Switches are defined in Module 19. Each is binary — there is no partial credit.
Master Sequence
KPI → System → Failure → Propagation → Economic Impact → Priority → Capability. The non-negotiable sequence through which every diagnosis must travel. No step is optional. No step can be performed before the preceding step is complete.
MSODA
Music School Operational Diagnostic Assessment. The proprietary framework for identifying system-level gaps in music school operations through a structured KPI-anchored sequence.
MSOI
Music School Operational Index. Composite score (0–100) representing overall systems health. Calculated as MI + EI + RI + SEI + CI. Each component measures a distinct systems domain.
Propagation
The causal domino chain through which a small structural failure travels — via human behavior change — to produce a large, measurable KPI impact. Propagation must be traced through behavior steps, not stated as direct cause-to-KPI.
Financial & Economic Terms
Economic Anchor
A conservative, formula-derived dollar amount quantifying the cost of a system failure. Delivered after the propagation chain is complete and accepted. Never estimated — always calculated with stated inputs.
LTV
Lifetime Value. Total profit generated by one student over their full enrollment tenure. Formula: Monthly Tuition × Gross Margin % × Months Retained. All three inputs must be stated when the LTV figure is used.
Leakage
Revenue structurally lost due to system failures — found in retention drops, conversion gaps, or utilization shortfalls. Leakage is not a loss from external market conditions. It is preventable through structural intervention.
Utilization Rate
The percentage of available lesson capacity currently generating delivered-lesson revenue. Formula: Delivered Lessons ÷ Scheduled Lessons. Below the network average = Scheduling System failure.
GAOS
GroovConnect Analyst Operating System. The five-layer intelligence environment (Training → Evaluation → Operational → Intelligence → Feedback) that connects every diagnostic, solution, and field outcome into a compounding institutional data asset. Without GAOS, the analyst's work exists as documents and conversations. With GAOS, every engagement compounds into structural intelligence.
"The KPI Signal shows a 15% drop in Retention. Our MSODA indicates a Standardization Failure in the Onboarding System. The Propagation shows this failure creates Experience Degradation across the 30-day post-enrollment window, resulting in a $40,000 Economic Leak. We must install the Engagement Tracking Capability and the Parent Portal Visibility Layer to stabilize the LTV and eliminate the Drift."

Nine Lexicon terms used correctly, in causal sequence, without repetition. This is the Gold Standard against which every Tier 3 diagnostic delivery is evaluated.
