Prioritization Model & Implementation Sequencing

Calculate priority scores with visible math. Sequence follows the 4-phase standard.

Priority = Impact × Frequency × Severity × Ease
Each factor rated 1–5  ·  Range: 1–625  ·  Higher scores lead
Impact (1–5)
Revenue, retention, or direct cost reduction. 5 = directly affects a primary KPI by more than 10 points.
Frequency (1–5)
How often the breakdown occurs and affects students. 5 = affects the majority of students every month.
Severity (1–5)
Degree of damage per occurrence. 5 = directly produces student churn or revenue loss.
Ease of Implementation (1–5)
Time, complexity, and resource requirements. 5 = deployable within one week with no additional infrastructure.
Rule: if multiple problems map to one GC capability, prioritize that capability regardless of individual scores. Deviating from the Standard Sequencing Order produces failure even when the solutions themselves are correct.
