5-Axis Scoring System

Reference: score descriptors per axis. Scorer: rate a trainee across all five axes and see the certification level.

Score the axis, not the person. If Diagnostic Accuracy is 3.0 and the threshold is 4.0, the trainee stays in remediation regardless of other scores. A single axis below threshold fires a remediation trigger — composite score does not override it.
Level 1
Composite ≥3.0
Level 2
Composite ≥4.0, no axis below 4.0
Level 3
Composite ≥4.5, no axis below 4.5
