GroovConnect OAS™ · Module 02, Section 5 · Chain Builder

Propagation chain — four-link builder

Present the failure node. Trainee builds all four links aloud — no pausing, no notes. Reveal one link at a time.

Four mandatory links: Failure → Behavior Change → Experience Degradation → KPI Impact. A human actor at every link. A chain that jumps from Behavior Change to KPI without passing through human experience is an assertion, not a causal argument.
Kill Switch: trainee connects Behavior Change directly to KPI Impact, skipping Experience Degradation. Stop. "What does the affected person feel between that behavior and the metric change? What decision does that feeling produce?"
