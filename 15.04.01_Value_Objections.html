Kill Switch: trainee quotes a dollar figure without showing the formula derivation, or uses an estimated figure. "The figure is the load-bearing element of the close." State: inputs → formula → output. Every time. If the operator checks the math and finds an estimate, trust in every other finding is lost.
The Evidence-Bridge: acknowledge the objection, clarify its source (Logical/Emotional/Practical/Identity), and reframe it using the diagnostic record. The bridge goes back to the evidence — every time. Not to persuasion, not to enthusiasm, not to additional claims.
