Execution Breakdown Taxonomy — 5 modes

Audit in sequence. Tap each mode to reveal its diagnostic question and structural response.

The sequence is mandatory. Motivation Failure can only be diagnosed after modes 1–4 have been fully audited and cleared. Skipping the sequence produces expensive non-solutions.
Kill Switch: trainee identifies Motivation Failure without first auditing Clarity, Ownership, System, and Capacity. Stop. "Walk me through modes 1–4. What does the process documentation say? Who is the named owner?" Only after each is audited and cleared can motivation be considered.
