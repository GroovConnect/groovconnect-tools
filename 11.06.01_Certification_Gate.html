Module 11 — Level 3 Capstone Requirement

The terminal gate. All five elements must pass. No partial credit. No rounding. No exceptions for strong performance in earlier modules.

The Capstone is evaluated against all five dimensions simultaneously. An analyst who produces a correct diagnosis but cannot communicate it clearly has not passed the Communication & Commitment dimension — regardless of Diagnostic Accuracy. All five are required. All five must score ≥4.0.
Scenario: Multi-location, multi-system failure. Three fractured systems. 120-minute time limit. Lucidchart only — no notes, no reference cards. Materials: Module 22 for the full operator narrative and reference standard.
