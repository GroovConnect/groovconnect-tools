Sim 2 — Signal vs. noise extraction

Read the statement verbatim. 90 seconds to identify 3 signals and 3 noise elements.

1:30
90-second window
Read verbatim — operator statement
"Look, I know there are some issues. My front desk person is great but gets overwhelmed. We had a big showcase last month — best one we've ever done, parents loved it. But we've lost maybe six or seven students since. My best teacher never turns in lesson notes but the kids who stay love her. I think the real problem is just the economy. Also, our piano waiting list has 18 names on it."
Trainee must identify: 3 KPI signals  ·  3 noise elements  ·  What the trainee must NOT do
Signals (3 required)
6–7 student churn post-showcaseRetention KPI signal — measurable, traceable.
Instructor not submitting lesson notesStandardization System — measurable on a spreadsheet.
Piano waitlist of 18 namesUtilization gap — capacity misallocation.
Noise (3 required)
"Great front desk person"Adjective — not a KPI.
"The economy is the problem"External attribution — not diagnosable.
"Best showcase ever"Narrative — no KPI anchor.
What the trainee must NOT do Accept "parents loved it" as evidence the showcase caused the churn · Accept the economy as a root cause without a traceable KPI mechanism · Ask whether the front desk person is "doing a good job" before naming a measurable metric.
