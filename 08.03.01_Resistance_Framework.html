Resistance Typology — 4 types

Resistance is information. Identify the type within 10 seconds. Never fight it — redirect it back to the KPI data.

Framework: Acknowledge → Clarify → Reframe → Resolve. The data is not the analyst's opinion — it does not require defense, only verification. When challenged, invite the operator to provide their own numbers and run the calculation live.

Kill Switch: trainee argues with the instructor playing the operator — defending the data, justifying the methodology, or escalating tone. Stop. "Arguing destroys influence. Invite the operator to provide their own numbers and recalculate live." The burden of correction must move to the operator, not be carried by the analyst.
