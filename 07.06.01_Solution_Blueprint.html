Solution Design Blueprint — 7 elements

Every proposed solution is incomplete until all seven elements are present. Tap each to reveal the gold standard example.

A recommendation missing any element cannot be implemented cleanly. The operator will encounter the missing information as a friction point during deployment and either stall or fill the gap incorrectly.
Element 7 — Automated Tracking Test: Can the system prove it is working from 1,000 miles away? If the answer requires asking anyone at the school, the tracking mechanism is missing. "We'll check" is not a tracking method. Required: a CRM report, automated dashboard flag, or time-stamped completion log that updates without human input.
