Integrity Rule: data quality is the prerequisite for system intelligence. An MSOI score calculated from incomplete lesson records is not a lower score — it is an invalid score.
Kill Switch: analyst diagnoses a retention issue using only Student records without referencing Match Score and Lesson data. A retention analysis without Match Score is structurally incomplete — it diagnosed an outcome without the mechanism that produces it. Require: Retention → Student + Lesson + Match Score.
