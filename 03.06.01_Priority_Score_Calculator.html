Priority Score — Magnitude × Frequency × Depth

Rate each factor 1–5 with a stated justification. Show the math. Score determines sequencing.

Priority Score = M × F × D
Magnitude × Frequency × Propagation Depth  ·  Range: 1–125
1 = minor/isolated/single-step  ·  5 = major/universal/4+ chain links  ·  Higher score addresses first
Priority sequencing
The score must be shown, not stated. "This is the highest priority" is not a Priority Score. "Magnitude 5, Frequency 5, Depth 4 — 5 × 5 × 4 = 100" is a Priority Score. Operators who challenge sequencing must be answered with the math.
