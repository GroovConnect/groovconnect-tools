Purpose & outcomes — Economic Impact Engine

What changes for the trainee between entering and leaving this module.

Enters with
A complete propagation chain terminating at a named KPI. No dollar figure. No formula.
→
Leaves with
A defensible dollar figure derived from a visible formula with stated assumptions — for any presented scenario.
Stalls at
Discomfort attaching large, specific figures to system failures. Tendency to soften, hedge, or qualify.
→
Installed
Clinical detachment. The number is the system's report card. It requires no apology and benefits from no softening.
Before
Multiple failures presented. No sequencing logic. Recommendations appear as a list.
→
After
Priority Score sequences every recommendation. Largest economic impact leads. Operator cannot reorder the sequence without providing data.
Certification pass standards
LTV formula

Revenue Waterfall
All four stages reconstructed from memory in correct sequence.

Breath Test
KPI → System → Failure → Impact → Dollar figure — one breath, no pauses.
The technical skill is the easy part. The harder part is delivering a $110,000 leakage figure with the same clinical neutrality as a $500 scheduling error. The number is not the analyst's opinion — it is the system's report card.
