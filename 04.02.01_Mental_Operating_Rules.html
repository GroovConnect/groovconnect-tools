Mental Operating Rules — 4 rules

Memorized in sequence. Recited before every simulation block. Tap each rule to see its trigger condition and required response.

These are not principles to aspire to — they are decision rules that activate automatically when the trigger condition appears. The goal is not to remember them from a card but to run them without thinking.
