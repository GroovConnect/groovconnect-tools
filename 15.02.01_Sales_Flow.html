GroovConnect OAS™ · Module 15, Section 2 · Sales Flow Stepper

5-Step Consultative Sales Flow

Hook → Diagnose → Reveal → Map → Commit. Steps 1–2 are purely diagnostic. No GC, no features, no product until Step 3.

Operators respond to authority differently than they respond to vendors. They answer questions honestly, reveal problems they would never reveal to a vendor, and accept conclusions derived from their own data.
Kill Switch: trainee opens the simulation by listing what GroovConnect can do before asking a single diagnostic question. Stop. "What question would you ask to open this conversation if you knew nothing about GC and were simply trying to understand whether this school has an operational intelligence problem?" That question is the Hook.
