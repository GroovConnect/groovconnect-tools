Diagnostic Stack & 5-Whys Protocol

Four layers to trace. Stop only at the Root Layer. The 5-Whys upgrade adds the Controllability Criterion.

The analysis is complete when the trainee can answer four consecutive questions: What is the symptom? → Which process produces it? → What structural design enables that process? → Why does the system behave this way? If the fourth answer is another symptom or a person's behavior — continue. The Root Layer has not been reached.
GC upgrade: stop at the first controllable cause, not the first explanation. Every "Why" must be: evidence-based · testable · controllable (terminating in a system or process the school can structurally change).
