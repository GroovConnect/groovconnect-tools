Kill Switch — trigger logic

Applies from Module 01 forward. Does not expire.

Kill Switch Trigger
Trainee attributes a system outcome to a person — "lazy," "unmotivated," or "unreliable" used as an explanation for a system failure
Stop the session immediately
"If that person were replaced tomorrow by someone equally motivated, would the outcome change?" — Pause. Wait for the answer.
No
Failure is structural
Reframe: "The system failed to filter, train, or incentivize. That is always where the diagnostic begins."
Yes
Possible Incentive or Selection failure
Still audit Clarity and System layers fully before addressing incentives.
This Kill Switch applies in every module from Module 01 forward — it does not expire. The goal is not to embarrass the trainee. The goal is to install the permanence test as an automatic checkpoint before any attribution is finalized.
