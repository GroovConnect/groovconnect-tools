Drift Detection & Recovery

Drift is structural — not motivational. Classify the lever, execute the protocol.

Drift definition: any adoption metric that fails to improve by 3 or more percentage points across 3 consecutive weekly reviews. The analyst does not improvise a response — they classify the lever and execute the protocol.
