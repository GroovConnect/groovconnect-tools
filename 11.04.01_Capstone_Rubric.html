Level 3 Capstone Rubric — 5 dimensions

Score each dimension 1–5. All five must score ≥4.0 for Level 3 certification. No rounding. No exceptions.

The Capstone is the terminal gate. No partial credit. A trainee who scores 4.8 on four dimensions but 3.2 on Scaling Architecture does not receive Level 3 certification. All five are required.
