Commitment Sequence — 4 steps

Agreement is not commitment. The sequence converts agreement into a documented operational action.

Nothing less than all four elements qualifies as a commitment. An incomplete commitment is operationally equivalent to no commitment. "Let's try and see" is a deferral with social permission — not a commitment.
Kill Switch: trainee softens the commitment language under operator pressure — "let's try and see how it goes" instead of a specific owner, date, and review point. Stop the drill. Extract a specific commitment or identify and address the resistance type preventing it. Does the conversation end with a named owner, specific task, specific date, and scheduled review? If any of these four are absent — the commitment is incomplete.
